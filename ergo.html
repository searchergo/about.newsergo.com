THE DASHBOARD FOR VALIDATING NEWS.

Ergo is on a mission to spark meaningful conversations by way of meaningful insights in the news. Powered by technology, Ergo uses machine learning and natural language processing to group together relevant stories and break down key context points to give you the full scope of what’s going on in our news today.

How does it work?

Ergo is different from a news aggregator or search engine.
Here’s a look at how Ergo takes you from one article to a full road map of that story.

Question

Let’s say you’re scrolling through a platform and see a news article that you find surprising or questionable. Search for that headline within the Ergo database.

Search

A digital fingerprint of that story is created through natural language processing and matched against hundreds of thousands of articles in the news database to find articles with a similar digital fingerprint.

Insight

A custom report outlines all the sources and the timeline of how that story has developed. Additional context tags help you understand how reliable or how different groups are discussing a single event.

Deeper Insights

Here's how Ergo highlights deeper context

so you can have the full picture.

Source Bias

What: Source bias meter ranges from dark blue (very liberal) to gray (neutral) to dark red (very conservative), and highlights the political leaning of a media source.

Why: See how stories are being discussed on the left and right.

Tags

What: Tags bring deeper insights to a story.

Why: Additional insights help you understand how reliable a story is.

Clusters

What: Swipe left and right to see how a range of media outlets are reporting on a single event.

Why: Understanding who and how media outlets are reporting on a story will help you navigate the validity or evolution of a story.

How do you determine a source's media bias?

Ergo works with Ad Fontes Media and AllSides, two non-profit news publication monitoring organization, to distinguish where a media source ranks in terms of political leaning and reliability of reporting. Learn more about their methodology here.

Is Ergo a neutral platform?

Ergo strives to generate more transparency in the news. We understand that biases in news leads to deeper polarization while also acknowledging the challenges in establishing a truly unbiased reporting system. That is why we demand transparency both from media sources and from our team.

How does Ergo determine what stories to show me on the homepage?

Ergo collects both stories from social outlets providing greater context to support the debates and it displays the stories most covered… prioritizes the stories that getting the most coverage.

How does Ergo determine whether a story is opinionated?

The Ergo algorithm uses sentiment analysis to learn and label language that indicates how polarized a statement is and flags those as being opinions.
